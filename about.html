Hi, I'm Lynda

"Beauty isn't just about looking good — it's about feeling it, too."

I've been passionate about beauty and aesthetics for over 15 years. What began as a deep love of the craft grew into a career devoted to helping others look and feel their absolute best. I founded LushLocks & Beauty because I believe every person deserves access to premium, medical-grade treatments in a warm, welcoming environment.

I know how much confidence can affect every aspect of your life — and I've seen firsthand the transformation that happens when a client leaves feeling truly beautiful. Whether it's correcting a long-standing insecurity, restoring a youthful glow, or simply treating yourself to some well-deserved self-care, I'm here for all of it.

I work exclusively from my studio inside Everyone Active in Romford, where I offer a calm, private, and professional environment. I pride myself on creating a genuinely personal experience — because your face, your skin, and your confidence are unique to you.
