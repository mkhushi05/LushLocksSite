Common Questions

How often should I have a facial?

For most skin types, a facial every 4–6 weeks is ideal. This aligns with your skin's cell turnover cycle and helps maintain consistent results. Monthly facials are Lynda's top recommendation for glowing, healthy skin year-round.

What's the difference between a regular facial and a HydraFacial?

A HydraFacial uses a specific device that combines cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, and serum infusion in one treatment — delivering more intense hydration and faster results. A regular facial uses hands-on techniques with premium products. Both are excellent — the HydraFacial just delivers a more intensive, immediate result.

What is a hot stone massage?

A hot stone massage uses smooth, heated basalt stones placed on key points of the body and incorporated into the massage technique. The heat penetrates deeper into the muscle tissue than hands alone, providing intense relaxation and relief from tension and stress.

Can I combine a facial with another treatment?

Absolutely — Lynda often recommends combining dermaplaning with a facial for outstanding results, or adding a jelly mask to any treatment. Contact Lynda via WhatsApp or book a consultation to design your perfect treatment combination.
