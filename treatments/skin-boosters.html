FAQs

✦

Common Questions

Is the treatment painful?

A topical numbing cream is applied before treatment to minimise discomfort. Most clients describe the sensation as mild pressure — very manageable and well-tolerated.

How many sessions do I need?

Many clients see noticeable improvement after their first session. For optimal results, Lynda recommends a course of 3 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart, then maintenance every 3–6 months.

Is PDRN safe?

PDRN is pharmaceutical-grade with decades of scientific research behind it. The products used by Lynda are medical-grade and FDA-approved — your safety is always the priority.

How long do results last?

Results typically last 3–6 months for a single session. A course of 3 sessions delivers considerably longer-lasting results. Maintenance sessions sustain your glow.

Who is suitable for this treatment?

Most adults of any skin type. Particularly effective for dehydrated skin, fine lines, dull complexion, or early signs of ageing. Not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding. A consultation will confirm suitability.
