FAQs

✦

Common Questions

Will my lips look unnatural or overdone?

Never — Lynda's philosophy is always natural enhancement. She works with your facial proportions to create results that look like the best version of you, not obviously "done". Less is more is always her approach on a first appointment.

Is it painful?

The lips are a sensitive area, but Lynda uses a numbing cream or dental block to make the treatment as comfortable as possible. Most clients rate discomfort very low.

How long will my lips be swollen?

Some swelling and possible bruising is normal for 24–72 hours. Final results are visible at 2 weeks once swelling has fully subsided. Wait for your 2-week review before judging results.

How long does lip filler last?

Most clients enjoy results for 6–12 months. This varies depending on the product used, your metabolism, and lifestyle factors. Top-up treatments can maintain your look.

Can lip filler be reversed?

Yes — hyaluronic acid filler can be dissolved quickly and safely using an enzyme called hyaluronidase if you ever want the filler removed or need a correction.
