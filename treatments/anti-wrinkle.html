FAQs

✦

Common Questions

Will I look frozen or unnatural?

Absolutely not — this is Lynda's speciality. She uses precise, conservative amounts and a thorough understanding of facial anatomy to achieve a completely natural, refreshed look. You should look like a better-rested version of yourself.

Does it hurt?

The needles used are ultra-fine and most clients describe it as a quick, mild sting. The procedure is very quick and well-tolerated. Numbing cream is available for sensitive clients.

When will I see results?

You'll usually see changes beginning within 3–5 days, with full results visible at 2 weeks. That's why Lynda offers a complimentary 2-week review to check results.

How long does it last?

Results typically last 3–5 months. With regular treatment over time, some clients find their results last longer as the muscles gradually weaken with repeated use.

What areas can be treated?

Common areas include: forehead lines, frown lines (11 lines), crow's feet, brow lift, bunny lines, and lip lines. Lynda will advise on the best areas for your goals during your consultation.
