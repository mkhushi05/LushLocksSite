What It Is

✦

Natural Hair
Restoration Science

Hair PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy is a scientifically advanced, non-surgical treatment that harnesses the healing power of your own blood to stimulate dormant hair follicles and encourage natural regrowth.

A small amount of your blood is drawn, then processed to concentrate the growth factors. This concentrated plasma is then injected directly into the scalp, stimulating follicles and reducing hair thinning — naturally, without foreign substances.

  • Reduces hair thinning and loss
  • Stimulates dormant follicles for natural regrowth
  • Uses your own blood — no foreign substances
  • Suitable for both men and women
  • Safe, non-surgical, and clinically proven

The Process

✦

What to Expect

01

Consultation

Lynda assesses your scalp health and hair loss pattern to determine if Hair PRP is the right treatment for you.

02

Blood Draw

A small amount of blood is taken from your arm — similar to a routine blood test. Completely safe and routine.

03

Centrifuge

Your blood is processed in a centrifuge to separate and concentrate the platelet-rich plasma (PRP).

04

Scalp Injections

The concentrated PRP is carefully injected across the scalp in areas of thinning or loss, stimulating follicle activity.

FAQs

✦

Common Questions

Is Hair PRP suitable for everyone?

Hair PRP is most effective for androgenetic alopecia (male/female pattern baldness) and diffuse thinning. It works best at early to moderate stages of hair loss when follicles are still active. Lynda will conduct a thorough consultation to assess suitability.

Will I see immediate results?

Hair PRP is a gradual treatment. Most clients notice reduced shedding after 2–3 sessions, with new growth becoming visible at 3–6 months. Patience and commitment to the full course is important for best results.

Is the blood draw painful?

The blood draw is the same as a standard blood test — minimal discomfort. The scalp injections are slightly more uncomfortable, but numbing cream is applied beforehand to minimise this.

How many sessions are required?

A course of 3 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart is the standard starting protocol. For significant hair loss, 6 sessions may be recommended, followed by quarterly maintenance to sustain results.

Can Hair PRP work alongside other hair treatments?

Yes — Hair PRP can be used alongside topical treatments such as minoxidil or combined with other scalp treatments. Lynda will discuss a comprehensive approach during your consultation.
