What It Is

✦

Reveal Your
Smoothest Skin

Dermaplaning is a safe, non-invasive exfoliation treatment that uses a sterile surgical scalpel to gently remove the top layer of dead skin cells and vellus (peach fuzz) hair from the surface of the skin.

The result is an instantly smoother, brighter, and more youthful-looking complexion. It also allows skincare products and makeup to penetrate and sit on the skin more effectively — the ultimate skin prep treatment.

  • Removes dead skin & vellus hair (peach fuzz)
  • Instantly smoother, brighter skin
  • Zero downtime — perfect before events
  • Enhances skincare product absorption
  • Safe for all skin types (except active acne)

FAQs

✦

Common Questions

Will my hair grow back thicker or darker?

This is a common myth — and it's completely false. Vellus (peach fuzz) hair is very different to terminal hair. Removing it with a blade will not change its colour, thickness, or growth rate. Your hair will grow back exactly as it was.

Is dermaplaning suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes — dermaplaning is actually very well-tolerated by sensitive skin. However, it's not recommended if you have active acne breakouts, rosacea flare-ups, or skin infections. Lynda will assess your skin during the consultation.

How often should I have dermaplaning?

Once every 4–6 weeks is ideal — this aligns with your skin's natural cell turnover cycle. Many clients book it monthly as part of their regular skincare routine.

Can I wear makeup immediately after?

We recommend waiting 24 hours before applying makeup to allow your freshly exfoliated skin to settle. After that, you'll notice your foundation applies more smoothly and flawlessly than ever!
