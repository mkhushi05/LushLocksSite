What It Is

✦

Your Skin's Natural
Renewal Process

Microneedling (collagen induction therapy) uses a precision device with ultra-fine needles to create thousands of micro-channels in the skin. This controlled micro-injury triggers the skin's natural healing response — producing fresh collagen and elastin for visibly improved skin quality.

The result is firmer, smoother, more even skin with noticeably reduced pores, acne scarring, fine lines, and uneven texture. When combined with our Skin Booster treatment, results are even more dramatic.

  • Reduces acne scarring and pigmentation
  • Minimises enlarged pores
  • Improves overall skin texture
  • Stimulates natural collagen & elastin
  • No harsh chemicals — your body does the work

FAQs

✦

Common Questions

Is microneedling painful?

With numbing cream, most clients find it very comfortable. You may feel a mild scratching or vibrating sensation. The numbing cream is applied 20–30 minutes beforehand for maximum comfort.

How many sessions do I need?

For general skin improvement, 3 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart is the recommended starting course. For acne scarring or more significant concerns, 6 sessions may be advised. Results continue to improve for several months after each session.

How long is the downtime?

Expect redness and mild sensitivity similar to a mild sunburn for 24–48 hours. Most clients return to work the following day. Avoid heavy makeup, sun exposure, and active skincare (acids, retinols) for 3–5 days.

Can microneedling help acne scars?

Yes — microneedling is one of the most effective non-surgical treatments for atrophic (indented) acne scars. It breaks down old scar tissue and encourages new collagen to fill and smooth the skin. Results build over multiple sessions.

What's the difference between standard and combo (microneedling + skin booster)?

The combo treatment infuses premium skin booster + PDRN directly into the micro-channels created during needling, dramatically amplifying hydration, healing and collagen stimulation. It's exceptional value at £100 and Lynda's most popular combination.
