Cancellation & Booking Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Booking & Deposits

A deposit may be required to secure some appointments. Deposits are non-refundable but may be transferred to a rescheduled appointment if sufficient notice is given (see below).

Cancellation Notice

We understand that life can be unpredictable. We respectfully ask that you provide as much notice as possible if you need to cancel or rearrange your appointment. Our minimum requirements are:

  • 48 hours or more: Full deposit transfer to a rescheduled appointment.
  • 24–48 hours: 50% of any deposit may be retained depending on circumstances.
  • Less than 24 hours / No-shows: The full deposit will be forfeited, and a new deposit may be required to rebook.

Late Arrivals

If you are running late, please let us know as soon as possible. We will always do our best to accommodate you, however appointments may be shortened or rescheduled to avoid disrupting other clients. Please note that the full appointment fee may still apply.

Rescheduling

You may reschedule your appointment up to 48 hours before your booking without penalty. To reschedule, please contact us by phone or WhatsApp.

Cancellation by LushLocks & Beauty

In the unlikely event that we need to cancel your appointment (e.g., due to illness or unforeseen circumstances), we will contact you as soon as possible to reschedule. Any deposits paid will be honoured in full for the rescheduled appointment.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 — LushLocks & Beauty by Lynda Smith

Who We Are

LushLocks & Beauty is operated by Lynda Smith and is located at Everyone Active, Gooshays Drive, Harold Hill, Romford, Essex RM3. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services or contact us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name, phone number, and email address (when you contact or book with us)
  • Health and medical information relevant to treatments (provided in consultation forms)
  • Communications via WhatsApp, email, or phone
  • Photographs (only with your explicit written consent, for before and after records)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide and manage your treatments and appointments
  • Contact you regarding bookings, results, or offers (only if you have consented)
  • Maintain accurate treatment and health records as required by professional standards
  • Comply with any applicable legal obligations

Data Sharing

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is kept strictly confidential. We do not share information with any third party unless legally required to do so.

Data Storage & Security

Your personal data is stored securely. Physical records are kept in a locked, secure location. Digital records are protected with appropriate security measures. We retain client records for a minimum of 7 years in line with professional guidelines.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at lyndasmithred3@gmail.com.

Photographs & Social Media

We will never use your photographs on social media or our website without your explicit prior written consent. If you have previously consented and wish to withdraw this consent, please contact us immediately.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. We may use basic session functionality only to ensure the website works correctly.

Health & Safety Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment

At LushLocks & Beauty, your health and safety is our highest priority. Lynda maintains the highest standards of cleanliness, sterilisation, and clinical safety in every treatment she performs.

Hygiene Standards

  • All tools and equipment are sterilised or single-use where applicable
  • Treatment areas are thoroughly cleaned and disinfected between each client
  • All needles and sharps are disposed of safely in accordance with regulations
  • PPE is used where appropriate

Contraindications

Treatments may not be suitable if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, have certain skin conditions, are taking specific medications, or have certain medical conditions. A full consultation will identify any contraindications before any treatment begins. Please disclose all relevant health information honestly.

Aftercare

Following your treatment, you will receive specific aftercare advice. It is essential that you follow this advice carefully to achieve the best results and minimise any risk of complications. Please contact Lynda immediately if you have any concerns after your treatment.

Allergies

Please inform us of any known allergies before your treatment, including allergies to specific ingredients or products. Patch tests will be carried out where appropriate. We will always do our utmost to accommodate your needs safely.
